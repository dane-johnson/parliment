Each player's goal is to advance her secret special interest by manipulation of parliamentary voting procedures.

A player may author bills to either advance her agenda or increase her ability to pass bills in the future.

All player should be a member of a specific political party, a group of individuals who they percieve as having common interests.

An authored bill will be a rule enacted within the game that will change the way money is distributed a specific round or the way parliment proceeds from then on.

The game must allow for times for table talk to create alliances towards common goals.

Players must be able to pass bills modifying such things as majorities, consigners, debate periods, tabling, riders, etc.
